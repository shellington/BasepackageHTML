Pizza & Peroni

1 June 2015

7pm

FREE!

Learn how to propel your business to the top of Google's rankings. Delivered by experts, Pizza & Peroni, Ph.Creative's free workshops will equip you with the latest tips and tricks in SEO, PPC, content marketing and social media.

We're Google's official Legacy Training Partners for Liverpool, so we know our onions. Speaking of onions, just ask if you want other pizza toppings.

Come and relax, network, enjoy Pizza and Peroni and discuss:

  • Advanced AdWords PPC strategy, keyword research and campaign delivery
  • SEO techniques, how to get to Google's top pages
  • Advanced online marketing techniques for high end business growth

Advanced social media campaign planning, online conversations and how Social Media platforms work together to deliver successful campaigns.

All our events will be followed by one-to-one workshops to suit individual businesses.
